FAQ

Common questions answered

Wallpaper doesn't change automatically?
Make sure the scheduler is enabled. Also disable battery optimization: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Wallpaper Scheduler ‚Üí Battery ‚Üí Unrestricted.
App crashes when selecting an image?
Check photo permissions: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Wallpaper Scheduler ‚Üí Permissions ‚Üí Photos ‚Üí Allow.
Doesn't work after phone restart?
Some phones require "Auto-start" permission. Open the app once after reboot to reactivate.
Can I have more than 2 wallpapers per day?
Currently morning and evening only. More time slots may come in future updates!
